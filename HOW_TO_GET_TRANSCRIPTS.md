## üîç How to Check if a Video Has Transcripts

### Method 1: Using YouTube Interface
1. Go to the YouTube video page
2. Click on the video player
3. Look for the **"CC"** (Closed Captions) button at the bottom of the player
4. If the button is present and clickable ‚Üí ‚úÖ Transcripts available
5. If the button is grayed out or missing ‚Üí ‚ùå No transcripts

### Method 2: Using YouTube's Transcript Feature
1. Open the YouTube video
2. Click the **"..." (More)** button below the video
3. Click **"Show transcript"**
4. If transcript appears on the right ‚Üí ‚úÖ Available
5. If option is missing or shows error ‚Üí ‚ùå Not available

## üö® Common Errors & Solutions

### Error: "No transcript available"
**Cause:** Video doesn't have captions enabled  
**Solution:** Try a different video with captions

### Error: "Transcript disabled"
**Cause:** Creator disabled captions for this video  
**Solution:** Choose another video

### Error: "Could not find video"
**Cause:** Invalid URL or private video  
**Solution:** Check URL and video accessibility

### Error: "Rate limit exceeded"
**Cause:** Too many requests in short time  
**Solution:** Wait a few seconds and try again
